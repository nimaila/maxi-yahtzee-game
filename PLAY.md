## Game Controls

### Setup Screen
- **Select Players**: Tap 1-4 to choose number of players
- **Enter Names**: Type player names (optional)
- **Customize Rules**:
  - Bonus Threshold: Points needed for 50pt bonus (default: 75)
  - Throws per Turn: Rerolls allowed (default: 3)
  - Dice Count: Number of dice (default: 6, range: 1-6)
- **Start Game**: Tap "NEW GAME" button

### Gameplay Screen
- **Roll Dice**: Tap "ROLL DICE" button
- **Hold Dice**: Tap individual dice to keep them (they glow when held)
- **Unhold Dice**: Tap held dice again to release
- **Score**: After final roll, tap a category to score
- **View Scorecard**: Swipe up to see all categories and scores

### Winner Screen
- **View Results**: See final scores and rankings
- **Play Again**: Tap "PLAY AGAIN" to start new game
- **View Stats**: Go to "MY STATS" tab to see history

## Gameplay Tips

### Scoring Strategy
- **Upper Section** (Ones-Sixes): Aim for 75+ points to unlock 50pt bonus
- **Lower Section**: Focus on high-value categories:
  - Maxi-Yahtzee (all same): 50 points
  - Tower (5 of a kind): 40 points
  - Full Straight (1-6): 40 points
  - Big Straight (2-6): 30 points

### Dice Strategy
- **Hold Pairs Early**: If you roll a pair, hold it for potential full house
- **Chase Straights**: 1-6 straight is worth 40 points
- **Save Chance**: Use "Chance" category as fallback if no good score

### Multi-Player Tips
- **Watch Opponents**: See their scores and adjust strategy
- **Bonus Race**: First to 75 in upper section gets 50pt bonus
- **Final Rounds**: Last 5 turns are critical—plan ahead

## Troubleshooting

### Game Won't Load
- **Browser**: Refresh the page or clear cache
- **Expo Go**: Restart the app or reinstall
- **APK**: Uninstall and reinstall the app

### Stats Not Saving
- **Browser**: Check if cookies/storage is enabled
- **Phone**: Ensure app has storage permission
- **Backup**: Stats are local-only; backup before uninstalling

### Audio Not Working
- **Browser**: Check volume and browser permissions
- **iPhone**: Disable "Silent Mode" (mute switch on side)
- **Android**: Check volume settings

### Animations Stuttering
- **Browser**: Close other tabs to free up memory
- **Phone**: Reduce animation speed in settings (if available)
- **Older Devices**: Some devices may have reduced performance
